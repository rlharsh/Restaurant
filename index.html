About Us

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality ingredients sourced locally, never shipped or frozen. We strive to use all organic plants / proteins that have been raised and do not contain antibiotics or other harmful chemicals.

Yan Clan Asian cuisine is curated by our professional chefs to ensure only the highest of standards of quality.

Cultural Inspiration

Our food is inspired by time-tested traditional Chinese recipies. Each of our dishes is hand-selected by our team of professional chefs.

Our foods are sourced locally and contain no steroids / antibiotics and we do not serve GMO foods / drinks.

Our Chinese food is truly an authentic experience that you can savor and enjoy with your whole family. Recipies date back for hundreds of years and our cooked by only the most select of professionaly awarded chefs, in order to bring you a truly authentic Chinese experience.

If you are interested in booking a reservation at our world class dining room please feel free to contact us.
